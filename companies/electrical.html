Folawiyo Electrical / Mechanical Limited

Yinka Folawiyo Electrical / Mechanical Limited was established in May 1997 to offer complete satisfaction about the quality, cost and general approach to any particular project. Our system of management is based on the principles of “Quality Management”.

The Company is managed by Patrick Spence, an Electrical Project Engineer. This executive has a combined overseas experience of more than twenty-three years in his field of specialization, previously gaining experience in Nigeria with one of the major mechanical and electrical organizations.

It is the company’s policy to employ only the best crasftmen available and to develop their skills with the necessary training and personal development enabling the creation of highly skilled and conscientious workforce.

Currently engaged on various construction projects in the Lagos environment, we are responsible for the complete design, supply and installation of all electrical installations comprising of 33KV, 11KV, 415V, 240V power, lighting, conduit, telephone, fire alarm, television, video entry, lightning protection system.

We endeavour wherever possible to source materials and equipment from indigenous manufacturers and suppliers in order to benefit the national economy and sustain employment within the boundaries of Nigeria.

This has enabled us to maintain projects on schedule whilst still giving good quality installations.
